Hey! I'm Jaeci. I'm a Creative Technoligies student at Illinois State University. I work in a variety of media. From more traditional drawing and painting, to mixed media and collage, to graphic design, to video art.

The subject matter of my art varies as much as the media. I enjoy making art as a criticism to current events, such as in my video piece, "hopscotch." On the other hand, I make pieces about events in my life. Art is a great way to sort out my feelings no matter how big or small the issue is. I also enjoy making art for no other reason than just making it! I think it's very calming and the feeling of seeing a piece come together is unmatched.
